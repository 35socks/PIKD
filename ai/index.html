PIKD's Stance on Generative AI in Music

As AI (Artificial Intelligence) Generated Content is becoming larger in the modern world, we would like to make a statement on our thoughts.

At PIKD, we take pride in our artist's releases and the hard work they put into them. We do not condone any Generative AI being used in a way to claim it as your own or monetise it.

Our procedure is as follows: If one of our artists try to upload AI Generated content using PIKD's services, the first time they attempt, their release will be denied. If it happens a second time, we will terminate the contract of said artist.

Please stay true to your craft as an artist. We do not put effort into promoting and releasing machine made music.
